Chocolate Chip Cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients:
    • In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and creamy.
    • Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    • Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Prepare Dry Ingredients:
    • In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined (dont overmix).

  4. Add Chocolate Chips: Fold in the chocolate chips or chunks.

  5. Form the Cookies:
    • Scoop dough into balls, about 1 - 2 tablespoons each, and place them on the baking sheet about 2 inches apart.

  6. Bake:
    • Bake for 8 - 10 minutes. The cookies may look slightly underdone but will set as they cool, resulting in a soft, chewy center.

  7. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips

  • For extra chocolatey cookies, sprinkle a few more chocolate chips on top before baking.
  • If you like your cookies softer, bake them for slightly less time; for a crispier texture, bake a bit longer.


Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar

  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar, packed

  • 2 large eggs

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour

  • 3/4 cup (75g) unsweetened cocoa powder

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1 cup (175g) chocolate chips or chunks (semi-sweet or dark)
